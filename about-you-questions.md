# About You — Question Bank (100 Questions)

These are the questions family members will be asked about themselves. Answers are free-text and will eventually be used to generate trivia games about each person.

## Childhood (10 questions)
1. Where were you born?
2. What is your earliest memory?
3. What was your favorite toy or game as a kid?
4. Did you have a childhood nickname? What was it?
5. What was your favorite subject in school?
6. Who was your best friend growing up?
7. What did you want to be when you grew up?
8. What's a funny or embarrassing story from your childhood?
9. What was your favorite thing to do after school?
10. Did you have any pets growing up? What were their names?

## Family (10 questions)
11. What's a tradition in your family that you love?
12. Who in your family are you most like, and why?
13. What's the best advice a family member ever gave you?
14. What's your favorite family vacation memory?
15. What holiday does your family go all-out for?
16. What's a dish that reminds you of home?
17. Who in your family makes you laugh the most?
18. What's something your parents always said to you?
19. What's a family story that gets retold at every gathering?
20. If you could relive one family moment, what would it be?

## Favorites (10 questions)
21. What's your all-time favorite movie?
22. What's a song that always makes you happy?
23. What's your favorite book or one that changed your life?
24. What's your comfort food?
25. What's your favorite season, and why?
26. What's your favorite way to spend a lazy Sunday?
27. What's your favorite holiday and why?
28. What's a TV show you could rewatch forever?
29. What's your favorite animal?
30. What's your favorite color and does it show up a lot in your life?

## Food & Cooking (10 questions)
31. What's your signature dish to cook?
32. What food could you eat every day and never get tired of?
33. What's the most adventurous thing you've ever eaten?
34. Do you have a secret family recipe? What is it for?
35. What's a food you absolutely can't stand?
36. What's your go-to order at a restaurant?
37. Coffee, tea, or something else? How do you take it?
38. What's the best meal you've ever had?
39. What snack is always in your kitchen?
40. If you could only eat one cuisine for the rest of your life, what would it be?

## Travel & Adventure (10 questions)
41. What's the most memorable place you've ever visited?
42. Where in the world would you most like to go?
43. What's the most adventurous thing you've ever done?
44. Do you prefer mountains, beach, or city?
45. What's a place that felt like home even though you'd never been there?
46. What's the longest road trip you've taken?
47. What's a funny travel story you love to tell?
48. Have you ever gotten lost somewhere? What happened?
49. What's your favorite thing about where you live now?
50. If you could live anywhere for a year, where would it be?

## Career & Education (10 questions)

52. What do you do for work, and do you enjoy it?
53. What's the most important thing you've learned from your career?
54. If money didn't matter, what would you do for a living?
55. Who has been your biggest mentor or professional influence?
56. What's a skill you're proud of that you taught yourself?
57. What's the hardest thing you've ever had to learn?
58. What accomplishment are you most proud of?
59. Did you go to college? What was your favorite thing about it?
60. What's something you know a lot about that might surprise people?
